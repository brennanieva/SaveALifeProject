Three Layers Of The Eye

“Human vision is fallible, but those mistakes that it makes can help us understand that wonderfully complex system”


The Middle Vascular Layer

The Posterior Choroid: A membrane that supplies all of the layers with blood
The Ciliary Body: A ring of muscle tissues that surrounds the lens

Famous part of this layer: The Iris
The colored part of the eye

The Lens
Light comes in through the cornea and pupil and hits the lens and focuses that light and projects it onto the retina
