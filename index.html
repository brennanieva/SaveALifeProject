Here's the Problem



Accourding to the National Security Council in 2018, A preventable-injury-related death occurs every 3 minutes in the United States. On average, this means there are 19 deaths and 5,390 medically consulted injuries every hour.


Red Cross Poll reports that 5% of adults are confident in their first aid skiils.




Lets Fix That

The fact is there's a first aid knowledge gap. It doesnt take a superhero to save the world. Taking the time to learn what to do in emergency puts you and everyone around you less at risk.
